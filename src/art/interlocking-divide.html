Interlocking Divide

Drawings inspireed by my bikes rides around St. Louis.

About the Project

These are works from a show I had at Third Degree Glass Factory in St. Louis in 2016. The drawings show many of the buildings I saw on numerous bike rides around the city of St. Louis. I wanted to create surreal dreamlike movement. I also wanted to show buildings together that are separated by the north/south divide of the city. By placing these structures together, does it show unity or does it show a fracture?
